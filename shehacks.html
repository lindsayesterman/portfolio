She Hacks

Developed community of 100+ female coders across 11 different countries, led weekly meetings teaching self-designed web development curriculum.

Mission

She Hacks is an organization dedicated to teaching women front-end web development and providing a community of women in web dev online. Members involved ask questions, talk about industry, discover new programming tools, and get to know other amazing female coders through our Slack Channel. We provide weekly free lessons teaching the fundamentals of software engineering.
